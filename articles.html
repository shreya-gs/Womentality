Controversy of Spanish Champions
for Womentality

Controversy struck after President of the Spanish Football Federation had forced himself upon Spanish World Cup Winner Jenni Hermioso, after the most recent triumph over England with a 1 goal deficit.

The ‘Los Toros’ refused to play for their beloved national team following this abashing controversy which was followed by a boycott and numerous protests worldwide to call for profound change. After weeks of continuous revolt, the Spanish Federation caved in and have sacked the president Luis Rubiales bringing great shame and chagrin to the beautiful game. To make amends, UEFA, and FIFA – the European governing bodies in control of a plethora of European competitions and creates legislation teams must abide by – sided with the player with the president of UEFA Alexander Ceferin stating that it was ‘inappropriate’, however would rather stay undiplomatic to not severe any ties with the Spanish Footballing Federation.
Spain’s team spirits were tarnished even preceding the World Cup, since a majority of players contradicted with the dated tactics and managerial style of now former head coach Jorge Vilda despite their 1-0 victory over England. Following this achievement however, in what is now seen as one of the most diabolical and laughable acts of recent memory, President Luis Rubiales kissed player Jenni Hermioso. The former president claims that the kiss was ‘mutual and with consent’, which fueled numerous weeks of infuriated and enraged fans around the globe as many were united in to bring about the resignation and leaving his position untenable due to heavy criticism.

Exasperated, Jenni Hermioso took to social media in a now deleted Instagram video response stating that the 33-year old felt ‘vulnerable’ and a ‘victim of aggression’, and slated the RFEF for manipulating and forcing certain players to choose sides which has caused two players to vacate the national team for ‘personal reasons.’ The RFEF prior to this pleaded UEFA to suspend Spanish teams which include global giants like Real Madrid, Barcelona, and Atletico Madrid to provoke fans and save Rubiales’ job.
Jenni Hermioso’s case has spread like wildfire internationally; a renowned case where it was the judiciary against the RFEF and thorough investigation from FIFA further backed with aggravated fans caused the resignation of the President. Numerous officials and high-ranking positions including all 19 of the Spanish regional presidents requested Rubiales to resign with surmounting pressure while offering unanimous support for interim president Pedro Rocha.

The President in an intransigent speech harshly claimed “fake feminism” which mortified a large number of feminists around the world further calling for his resignation and even went as far as conveying death threats. A lawyer by the name Paloma Torres said to CNN ‘We are all very angry at this because all women have suffered some kind of abuse’. The 29-year old had unwavering backing towards Jenni Hemioso and is an advocate for women abuse and sexual assault globally.
